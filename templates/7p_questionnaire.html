Concerned Health Issues

1. Do you have food allergies (e.g. eggs, peanuts, seafood, etc.)? 您有食物過敏(例如: 雞蛋、花生、海鮮等)嗎?


2. Do you have environmental allergies (e.g. pollen, dust mites, etc.)? 您有環境過敏(例如: 花粉、塵蟎等)嗎?


3. Do you have ever had eczema? 您是否曾經患有濕疹?


4. Do you suffer from allergic rhinitis or asthma? 您是否患有鼻敏感或哮喘?


5. What was your birth delivery mode? 您在出生的時候是剖腹產還是順產?


6. Have you taken probiotics supplements over the past three months? 您在過去三個月內是否有服食益生菌或相關補充品?


7. Have you taken antibiotics in the past month? 您在過去一個月是否有服食抗生素或相關的藥物?


8. Do you have persistent diarrhea? If yes, please specify the frequency (e.g. how many times of excretion a day). 您有沒有持續的腹瀉? 如有，請註明排便頻率(例如一天多少次)


9. Do you have persistent constipation? If yes, please specify the frequency (e.g. how many times of excretion a day). 您有沒有持續的便秘? 如有，請註明排便頻率(例如一天多少次)


10. Do you have an uncomfortable stomach? If yes, please specify the frequency. 您有胃部不適的問題嗎? 如有，請註明頻率


11. Do you often get colds and flus? If yes, please specify the frequency. 您會經常患傷風感冒嗎? 如有，請註明頻率

13. How often do you eat high fat or high cholesterol food? 您會進食含高脂肪或高膽固醇的食物嗎?


14. How often do you eat snacks or desserts? 您會進食糖類或甜食嗎?


15. Do you have high blood pressure, high blood cholesterol/triglycerides or high blood sugar? 您有高血壓、高膽固醇/高血脂或高血糖嗎?


16. Do you have metabolic arthritis? 您患有痛風嗎?


17. Do you have an autoimmune disease (e.g. rheumatoid arthritis, systemic lupus erythematosus, etc.)? 您是否患有自體免疫疾病(例如: 類風濕性關節炎、全身性紅斑狼瘡等)?
